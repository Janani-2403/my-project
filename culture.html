Kerala

Kerala is known for classical dance forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and rich temple arts. Ayurveda is a vital part of its wellness culture.

Popular Dishes:

  • Kerala Sadya
  • Appam with Stew
  • Fish Curry
  • Puttu & Kadala Curry

Kanniyakumari

Kanyakumari's culture is a vibrant blend of ancient traditions, religious devotion to Goddess Kanyakumari, and a unique connection to the meeting of three seas.

Popular Dishes:

  • Meen Kuzhambu
  • Nethili (anchovy) curry
  • Pazham Pori
  • Banana Chips

Kashmir

Kashmiri culture is a vibrant blend of traditions influenced by Hinduism, Islam, Sikhism, and Buddhism, known for its rich heritage, crafts, and unique cuisine.

Popular Dishes:

  • Rogan Josh (lamb curry)
  • Yakhni (yogurt-based curry)
  • Dum Aloo
  • Wazwan

Ooty

Ooty's culture is a blend of Tamil traditions, British colonial influence, and the unique customs of the Toda tribe, with a strong emphasis on natural beauty and hospitality.

Popular Dishes:

  • Neer Dosa
  • Bisi Bele Bath
  • Chicken Chettinad
  • Jackfruit Curry

Mysore

Mysore is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, encompassing grand palaces, vibrant festivals like Dasara, distinctive arts like Mysore painting and silk, and its historical role as the capital of the Wodeyar kings.

Popular Dishes:

  • Mysore Pak
  • Mysore Masala Dosa
  • Rava Kesari
  • Maddur Vada

New Delhi

Delhi boasts a vibrant, cosmopolitan culture shaped by centuries of history, diverse communities, and a unique blend of traditions and modernity.

Popular Dishes:

  • Chole Bhature
  • Butter Chicken
  • Jalebi
  • Nihari
