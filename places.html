Munnar

Munnar is a scenic hill station famous for its tea gardens and cool climate.

Alleppey

Enjoy the peaceful backwaters and stay in houseboats in Alleppey.

Kochi

Explore Kochi’s colonial buildings and famous Chinese fishing nets.

Vagamon

Vagamon is a serene hill station in Kerala, India, known for its lush tea gardens,
pine forests, and meadows, offering a tranquil escape with cool, pleasant weather.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is a renowned coastal town in Kerala, India, famous for its three
crescent-shaped beaches and tranquil waters, making it a popular destination
for relaxation and water activities.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake is a famous urban lake in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir,
known for its scenic beauty, houseboats, and Mughal gardens.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam is a picturesque town in Kashmir, known as the "Valley of Shepherds",
famous for its scenic beauty, lush meadows, and as a base for the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.

Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a magnificent historical palace
and former royal residence of the Wodeyar dynasty in Mysore, Karnataka,
India, known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture.
