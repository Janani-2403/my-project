Discover Kerala

Kerala is a state on the southwestern coast of India, known for its serene beaches, backwaters,
lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. Start exploring now!

Discover Kanniyakumari

Kanyakumari, at India's southernmost tip, is a coastal town where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal,
and Indian Ocean converge, known for its unique sunset and sunrise views, temples, and the Vivekananda Rock Memorial.

Discover Kashmir

Kashmir is a region in the Himalayas known for its stunning natural beauty, including valleys,
lakes, and mountains, and is a subject of territorial dispute between India, Pakistan, and China.

Discover Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a picturesque hill station in Tamil Nadu, India,
renowned for its tea plantations, scenic beauty, and pleasant climate.

Discover Mysore

Mysore, also known as Mysuru, is a city in Karnataka, India, famous for its palaces, silk, sandalwood,
and cultural heritage, earning it the nickname "City of Palaces".

Discover New Delhi

New Delhi is India's capital, a bustling metropolis known for its historical sites, diverse culture, and as a major political and commercial hub.
