DRUNG WATERFALL

Drung Waterfall, also known as Drang Waterfall, is a stunning
natural attraction located near Gulmarg in Kashmir, India.
It's particularly famous for its frozen spectacle during
winter, where the cascading water turns into a sheet of ice.
The waterfall is located in the village of Drung, near
Tangmarg, and is easily accessible from both Srinagar and Gulmarg.

Frozen Beauty:

During winter, the waterfall freezes, creating a breathtaking scene of solid ice formations hanging from the rocks.

Location:

Drung Waterfall is situated in the Tangmarg tehsil of Gulmarg, about 16 km from Gulmarg and 42 km from Srinagar.
